⚪ JOHTO REGION
01 NEW BARK TOWN — THE BEGINNING 🏠
  • ▶Name your character. Professor Elm calls you to his lab in New Bark Town.
  • ⭐Choose your starter from Elm's lab: Chikorita (Grass), Cyndaquil (Fire), or Totodile (Water).
  • ▶Elm asks you to deliver an egg to Mr. Pokémon on Route 30. Head north through Route 29.
  • ▶A red-haired kid (your rival, Silver) steals a starter from Elm's lab — you'll fight him shortly.
  • ⭐Reach Mr. Pokémon's house on Route 30. He gives you the Mystery Egg and you meet Professor Oak, who gives you the Pokédex.
  • 🔴Rival battle on Route 29 on the way back. Beat him and note his name from a wanted poster.
  • ⭐Return the egg to Elm — he gives you 5 Poké Balls and unlocks your journey.

02 VIOLET CITY — GYM 1: FALKNER 🪶
FALKNER — ZEPHYR BADGE
Flying type · Pidgey Lv9 · Pidgeotto Lv13
  • ▶Head west through Routes 29 and 46, then north through Routes 36 and 32 (or directly via Route 31 to Violet City).
  • ▶Visit Sprout Tower north of Violet City for XP and to find HM05 Flash at the top. Rival Silver is also here.
  • 🔴Beat Falkner. Rock and Electric moves are ideal. Totodile users: grind to Lv15+ before this fight.
  • ⭐Zephyr Badge + TM51 (Roost). The Mystery Egg hatches into Togepi after enough steps — pick it up from Elm's assistant.

03 AZALEA TOWN — SLOWPOKE WELL + GYM 2: BUGSY 🐛
BUGSY — HIVE BADGE
Bug type · Metapod Lv14 · Kakuna Lv14 · Scyther Lv16
  • ▶Head south through Route 32 (catch Mareep!) and through Union Cave to reach Azalea Town.
  • ⭐Team Rocket is cutting Slowpoke tails in Slowpoke Well. Clear them out to unlock the gym.
  • 🔴Rival battle outside the gym after clearing the Well. Then beat Bugsy — Fire and Flying wreck him. Watch out for Scyther's U-turn.
  • ⭐Hive Badge + TM89 (U-turn). Head east through Ilex Forest next.
  • 💧Help the Charcoal Man's apprentice in Ilex Forest to receive HM01 Cut.

04 GOLDENROD CITY — GYM 3: WHITNEY 🌸
WHITNEY — PLAIN BADGE
Normal type · Clefairy Lv17 · Miltank Lv19
  • ▶Pass through Ilex Forest and north through Routes 34 to Goldenrod City.
  • ⭐Goldenrod has a massive Department Store — stock up on items, TMs, and evolution stones.
  • ⭐Get the Bicycle for free from the Bike Shop (just promise to advertise for them).
  • 🔴Beat Whitney. Miltank's Rollout + Milk Drink is infamous — bring a Rock or Steel type and chip it down fast. Fighting moves are super effective.
  • ⭐Plain Badge + TM45 (Attract). Whitney cries then gives you the badge after a moment.
  • ⭐National Park is nearby (Route 35/36) — Bug-Catching Contest runs Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday. Great for rare Pokémon.

05 ECRUTEAK CITY — BURNED TOWER + GYM 4: MORTY 👻
MORTY — FOG BADGE
Ghost type · Gastly Lv21 · Haunter Lv21 · Haunter Lv23 · Gengar Lv25
  • ▶Head north and east to Ecruteak City. Visit the Burned Tower with your rival Silver.
  • ⭐In the Burned Tower basement, you encounter and awaken the three legendary beasts — Raikou, Entei, and Suicune — who flee into the wild.
  • ⭐Meet the Kimono Girls in Ecruteak Dance Hall — you'll fight all five throughout the game and they play a role in the endgame legendary encounter.
  • 🔴Beat Morty. Dark-type moves are super effective, but there are few in Johto early on. Use Psychic or high-attack Normal moves. Gengar is tricky — it's Ghost/Poison.
  • ⭐Fog Badge + TM30 (Shadow Ball). Surf HM accessible now.
  • 💧Get HM03 Surf from the Dance Hall in Ecruteak after beating all Kimono Girls you can access now (some are spread around Johto).

06 OLIVINE CITY — LIGHTHOUSE + GYM 6: JASMINE ⚙️
JASMINE — MINERAL BADGE
Steel type · Magnemite Lv30 · Magnemite Lv30 · Steelix Lv35
  • ▶Head west from Ecruteak to Olivine City (Routes 38/39). Jasmine is at the Lighthouse tending to a sick Pokémon — Ampharos.
  • ⭐Jasmine won't battle until you get medicine from Cianwood City across the sea. Use Surf to cross Route 40/41.
  • 💧Get HM02 Fly from a person in Cianwood City. Beat Chuck's gym while you're there.
  • 🔴Return medicine to Jasmine. Now she'll battle. Fire and Fighting destroy Steel types — Feraligatr/Croconaw can use Water + Fighting coverage.
  • ⭐Mineral Badge + TM23 (Iron Tail).

07 MAHOGANY TOWN — LAKE OF RAGE + GYM 7: PRYCE + TEAM ROCKET 🧊
PRYCE — GLACIER BADGE
Ice type · Seel Lv27 · Dewgong Lv29 · Piloswine Lv31
  • ▶Head east from Ecruteak to Mahogany Town (Routes 42/43). A man blocks the gym — first handle the Lake of Rage.
  • ⭐Surf north to the Lake of Rage. A Red Gyarados is creating chaos — catch it (it's a guaranteed shiny!), then meet Lance.
  • ⭐Team up with Lance to raid Team Rocket's hideout in Mahogany Town. Clear three floors of grunts and two executives.
  • 🔴Beat Pryce. Fire and Fighting work well. Navigate the ice-floor puzzle to reach him.
  • ⭐Glacier Badge + TM72 (Avalanche).

08 GOLDENROD CITY (RETURN) — TEAM ROCKET RADIO TOWER 📻
  • ⭐After beating Pryce, Team Rocket takes over Goldenrod's Radio Tower. Head back to the city.
  • ▶Infiltrate the Radio Tower (5 floors of grunts). First grab the Basement Key from the Underground Tunnel to enter the Warehouse below.
  • ⭐Clear the Underground Warehouse to get the Card Key, which unlocks doors in the Radio Tower.
  • 🔴Fight Executive Archer at the top. He uses Houndoom and other Dark types — bring Fighting.
  • ⭐The Director is saved and gives you the Rainbow Wing (HG) or Silver Wing (SS). This allows you to encounter Ho-Oh or Lugia at their respective locations.
💡 This is the major Team Rocket story payoff — their second and final defeat. After this, head to Blackthorn City for the last Johto gym.
09 BLACKTHORN CITY — ICE PATH + GYM 8: CLAIR 🐉
CLAIR — RISING BADGE
Dragon type · Dragonair Lv37 · Dragonair Lv37 · Dragonair Lv38 · Kingdra Lv41
  • 💧Navigate the Ice Path (east of Mahogany) using Strength and Surf. Pick up HM07 Waterfall inside.
  • 🔴Beat Clair — one of the toughest gym leaders. Ice moves hit Dragonair hard but Kingdra only takes neutral damage from Ice and Dragon. Bring your best team (Lv40+).
  • ⭐Clair refuses to give the badge — go to the Dragon's Den to prove yourself. Answer the elder's questions correctly.
  • ⭐Rising Badge + TM59 (Dragon Pulse). The Dragon's Den also has a gift Dratini (or Dragonair if you answered perfectly).

10 BELL TOWER / WHIRL ISLANDS — HO-OH & LUGIA 🌟
  • ⭐Before the Pokémon League, defeat all 5 Kimono Girls (scattered across Johto — Violet, Goldenrod, Ecruteak ×2, Blackthorn). They then gather at the legendary Pokémon's location.
  • ⭐HeartGold: Kimono Girls summon Ho-Oh at the Bell Tower in Ecruteak. Climb all 10 floors. Ho-Oh is at Lv45.
  • ⭐SoulSilver: Kimono Girls summon Lugia at Whirl Islands. Navigate to the deepest room. Lugia is at Lv45.
  • 🔴Battle legendary: bring Ultra Balls, Dusk Balls, and a Pokémon that can paralyze or sleep. False Swipe it to 1 HP first.

11 VICTORY ROAD + JOHTO ELITE FOUR & LANCE 🏆
  • ▶Head east from New Bark Town (use Surf) to reach Victory Road. You need all 8 Johto badges.
  • ▶Victory Road is short but has strong wild Pokémon. Aim for Lv40–45 before the Elite Four.
  • 🔴Will (Psychic) — Xatu ×2, Jynx, Slowbro, Exeggutor. Use Dark, Ghost, and Bug moves.
  • 🔴Koga (Poison) — Ariados, Venomoth, Forretress, Muk, Crobat. Use Ground, Psychic, and Fire.
  • 🔴Bruno (Fighting) — Hitmontop, Hitmonlee, Hitmonchan, Machamp ×2. Use Psychic and Flying.
  • 🔴Karen (Dark) — Umbreon, Vileplume, Murkrow, Gengar, Houndoom. Use Fighting and Fairy moves.
  • 🔴Champion Lance (Dragon) — Gyarados, Dragonite ×3, Aerodactyl, Charizard. Ice Beam is essential. Aim for Lv50+.

💡 Ice Beam (buy from the Goldenrod Dept. Store) will carry you through Lance's entire team. Have at least one fast Ice-type move user going in.
🔴 KANTO REGION — POST-GAME
12 KANTO — ALL 8 KANTO GYMS 🗺️
  • ⭐After the credits, you wake up in New Bark Town. Take the ferry from Olivine to Vermilion City, Kanto.
  • ▶All 8 Kanto gym leaders are waiting — in any order. Their Pokémon are much higher level (Lv50–70+), so grind before taking them on.
  • 🔴Brock (Rock) — Graveler Lv52, Rhyhorn Lv54, Omastar Lv55, Onix Lv51, Kabutops Lv53. Water/Grass.
  • 🔴Misty (Water) — Golduck Lv49, Quagsire Lv49, Lapras Lv52, Starmie Lv54. Electric/Grass.
  • 🔴Lt. Surge (Electric) — Raichu Lv51, Electrode ×2 Lv47/53, Magneton Lv51, Electabuzz Lv53. Ground.
  • 🔴Erika (Grass) — Jumpluff Lv51, Tangela Lv46, Victreebel Lv46, Bellossom Lv46. Fire/Ice/Flying.
  • 🔴Janine (Poison) — Crobat Lv53, Weezing Lv51, Ariados Lv47, Venomoth Lv50, Weezing Lv53. Ground/Psychic.
  • 🔴Sabrina (Psychic) — Espeon Lv58, Mr. Mime Lv53, Alakazam Lv55, Jynx Lv57. Dark/Ghost/Bug.
  • 🔴Blaine (Fire) — Magcargo Lv54, Rapidash Lv52, Magmar Lv54. Water/Rock/Ground.
  • 🔴Blue (Mixed) — Pidgeot Lv58, Rhydon Lv58, Arcanine Lv58, Exeggutor Lv58, Gyarados Lv58, Alakazam Lv58. Varied — bring your best team.

13 MT. SILVER — RED (THE TRUE FINAL BOSS) 🔥
  • ⭐After collecting all 16 badges (8 Johto + 8 Kanto), Professor Oak gives you access to Mt. Silver via Route 28.
  • ▶Mt. Silver is a brutal optional dungeon. Wild Pokémon are Lv40–60+. Grind extensively — you want Lv70–80 for the final boss.
  • 🔴RED — the true final boss and one of the hardest fights in all of Pokémon. He does not speak.
