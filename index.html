01 PALLET TOWN — THE BEGINNING 🏠
  • ▶Name your character and rival (Gary). Your rival's name defaults to "Gary" or "Blue" canonically.
  • ▶Attempt to leave town north — Professor Oak stops you and takes you to his lab.
  • ⭐Choose your starter Pokémon from Oak's desk. Your choice determines your rival's counter-pick.
  • ▶Rival immediately challenges you to your first battle — win or lose, it doesn't affect the story.
  • ⭐Receive the Pokédex from Oak. Your adventure officially begins.
🎮 Starter Choice

💡 Bulbasaur trivializes gyms 1 & 2. Charmander makes early gyms rough but dominates mid-game.
02 ROUTE 1 → VIRIDIAN CITY → ROUTE 2 🌿
  • ▶Walk north through Route 1. Grind if needed — wild Rattata and Pidgey give decent early XP.
  • ▶Reach Viridian City. The Poké Mart won't sell to you yet — deliver Oak's Parcel first.
  • ⭐Return to Pallet Town, deliver the parcel to Oak. Receive the Town Map from your rival's sister.
  • ▶Head back to Viridian City. The Gym is locked for now — skip it.
  • ▶Pass through Viridian Forest on Route 2. The forest has your first Bug-type encounters.

03 PEWTER CITY — GYM 1: BROCK 🪨
BROCK — BOULDER BADGE
Rock/Ground type · Geodude Lv12 · Onix Lv14
  • ▶Visit the Pewter Museum of Science (north of town) for lore, but it's optional.
  • 🔴Challenge Brock. Grass and Water moves wreck him. Charmander users: grind Mankey on Route 22 for Low Kick.
  • ⭐Earn Boulder Badge + TM39 (Rock Tomb). Flash HM can now be used outside battle.

04 CERULEAN CITY — GYM 2: MISTY 💧
MISTY — CASCADE BADGE
Water type · Staryu Lv18 · Starmie Lv21
  • ▶After arriving, go north to Nugget Bridge on Route 24. Battle 5 trainers for a Nugget (sell for cash), then face a Team Rocket grunt.
  • ⭐On Route 25 (past Bill's house), Bill will be in a transporter — help him revert. He gives you an SS Ticket (needed later).
  • 🔴Beat Misty. Grass and Electric types are key. Starmie hits hard — be at least Lv25.
  • ⭐Cascade Badge + TM03 (Water Pulse). Cut HM can now be used outside.

05 VERMILION CITY — SS ANNE + GYM 3: LT. SURGE ⚡
LT. SURGE — THUNDER BADGE
Electric type · Voltorb Lv21 · Pikachu Lv18 · Raichu Lv24
  • ▶Travel south through Routes 5 & 6 (or the Underground Tunnel) to reach Vermilion City.
  • ⭐Board the SS Anne with your SS Ticket. Battle trainers for XP, then find the Captain on the top deck.
  • ⭐Help the seasick Captain — he gives you HM01 Cut. Teach it to a Pokémon immediately.
  • ▶The SS Anne departs after you leave. Use Cut to enter Surge's gym.
  • 🔴Find the two hidden trash cans (one reveals a switch, the second is adjacent) to open Surge's door. Ground-types dominate this fight.
  • ⭐Thunder Badge + TM34 (Shock Wave). Fly HM can now be used outside.

06 CELADON CITY — GYM 4: ERIKA + TEAM ROCKET HQ 🌈
ERIKA — RAINBOW BADGE
Grass type · Victreebel Lv29 · Tangela Lv24 · Vileplume Lv29
  • ▶Head west through Routes 7 and the Underground Tunnel or Rock Tunnel (use Flash to navigate) from Route 10.
  • ⭐Celadon Game Corner — check behind the Team Rocket poster for a hidden switch to access the Rocket HQ basement.
  • ▶Clear the Rocket HQ (4 floors, multiple admins). Fight your way to the boss: Giovanni.
  • 🔴Beat Giovanni in Rocket HQ — he drops the Silph Scope. Fire, Ice, and Poison beat Erika.
  • ⭐Rainbow Badge + TM19 (Giga Drain). Strength HM can now be used outside.
  • ⭐Game Corner Prizes: Porygon, Scyther, and Pinsir available here with coins (Scyther is LeafGreen exclusive).

07 LAVENDER TOWN — POKÉMON TOWER 👻
  • ▶Head east to Lavender Town (Routes 8 → 7 → 12, or through Rock Tunnel).
  • ▶Enter Pokémon Tower. Without the Silph Scope, you can't identify Ghost-type encounters — get the Scope from Celadon first.
  • ⭐Fight through seven floors of ghosts and Rocket Grunts. You'll encounter the ghost of a Marowak on floor 5 — you cannot catch it, just defeat it.
  • 🔴Rescue Mr. Fuji from Team Rocket on the top floor. He gives you the Poké Flute.

08 FUCHSIA CITY — GYM 5: KOGA + SAFARI ZONE 💜
KOGA — SOUL BADGE
Poison type · Koffing Lv37 · Muk Lv39 · Koffing Lv37 · Weezing Lv43
  • ▶Use the Poké Flute to wake Snorlax blocking Route 12 (south of Lavender) or Route 16 (west of Celadon). Catch it — it's a great team member.
  • ▶Head south/west to Fuchsia City. Koga's gym has invisible walls — navigate carefully.
  • 🔴Beat Koga. His Pokémon use Smokescreen and Toxic — bring Antidotes and status healers. Psychic-types destroy him.
  • ⭐Soul Badge + TM27 (Return). Surf HM can now be used outside.
  • ⭐Safari Zone — north of Fuchsia. Get HM03 Surf and HM04 Strength from the Warden (give him his Gold Teeth, found in the Zone).

09 SAFFRON CITY — SILPH CO. + GYM 6: SABRINA 🔮
SABRINA — MARSH BADGE
Psychic type · Kadabra Lv38 · Mr. Mime Lv37 · Venomoth Lv38 · Alakazam Lv43
  • ▶Head to Saffron City (center of Kanto). Team Rocket has taken over — you can't enter Silph Co. yet without clearing the path.
  • ⭐Navigate Silph Co.'s 11 floors using teleport pads. Find the Card Key on 5F to open all locked doors.
  • 🔴Rival battle on 7F. Then face Team Rocket admin on top floors. Final boss: Giovanni again (Lv45 Persian, Lv42 Rhyhorn, Lv44 Kangaskhan, Lv50 Nidorino, Lv50 Nidoqueen).
  • ⭐After beating Giovanni, the Silph Co. President gives you the Master Ball — save it for Mewtwo or a roaming legendary.
  • 🔴Beat Sabrina. Bug and Ghost types counter Psychic (though Ghost doesn't work in Gen 1 as expected). Dark types aren't in Gen 1 — use high-HP tanky Pokémon.
  • ⭐Marsh Badge + TM04 (Calm Mind). Surf can now pass the water south of the gym area.
🎯 Key Pokémon Around Saffron

💡 The Lapras gift on Silph Co. 7F is a powerful Water/Ice Pokémon — a great Surf user for your team. Hitmonlee vs Hitmonchan: Lee has higher attack and kick moves; Chan has better special defense and elemental punches.
10 CINNABAR ISLAND — POKÉMON MANSION + GYM 7: BLAINE 🌋
BLAINE — VOLCANO BADGE
Fire type · Growlithe Lv42 · Ponyta Lv40 · Rapidash Lv42 · Arcanine Lv47
  • 💧Teach Surf and head south from Pallet Town (or west from Fuchsia along Routes 19/20). Surf to Cinnabar Island.
  • ⭐Blaine's Gym is locked — find the Secret Key inside the Pokémon Mansion (the burned building north of the gym).
  • ▶Navigate the Mansion's 4 floors, reading journals (they detail Mewtwo's creation backstory). The Secret Key is on Basement 1.
  • 🔴Beat Blaine with Water, Rock, or Ground moves. Arcanine at Lv47 is a tough final hurdle.
  • ⭐Volcano Badge + TM38 (Fire Blast).
  • ⭐Optional: Talk to the scientist in the Lab on Cinnabar for the Old/Good/Super Rod upgrades and fossil resurrection (Dome → Kabuto, Helix → Omanyte).

11 VIRIDIAN CITY — GYM 8: GIOVANNI 🌍
GIOVANNI — EARTH BADGE
Ground type · Rhyhorn Lv45 · Dugtrio Lv42 · Nidoqueen Lv44 · Nidoking Lv45 · Rhydon Lv50
  • ▶The Viridian Gym is finally open. Navigate the spinning tile floor maze.
  • 🔴Beat Giovanni — the final Rocket leader. Water, Grass, and Ice moves destroy his Ground team. Rhydon at Lv50 is the real threat.
  • ⭐Earth Badge + TM26 (Earthquake). All HMs can now be used freely.
  • ▶Giovanni dissolves Team Rocket after this defeat and flees — ending their criminal organization.
💡 Giovanni is the easiest 8th gym leader in the series if you have a Water or Ice type. Any fully-evolved Water Pokémon (Lapras, Blastoise, Vaporeon) will make this trivial.
12 VICTORY ROAD + POKÉMON LEAGUE 🏆
  • ▶Head north through Route 22 → Route 23 → Victory Road. You need all 8 badges to pass the badge checks.
  • ▶Victory Road is a 3-floor cave requiring Surf, Strength, and Rock Smash to navigate. Grind heavily here — aim for Lv50–55+ before the Elite Four.
  • 🔴Lorelei (Ice) — Dewgong, Cloyster, Slowbro, Jynx, Lapras. Use Electric and Grass moves.
  • 🔴Bruno (Fighting) — Onix ×2, Hitmonchan, Hitmonlee, Machamp. Use Psychic and Flying.
  • 🔴Agatha (Ghost) — Gengar ×2, Haunter, Arbok, Gengar. Use Psychic and Ground.
  • 🔴Lance (Dragon) — Gyarados, Dragonair ×2, Aerodactyl, Dragonite. Ice Beam is the MVP move here.
  • 🔴Champion: Your Rival — team varies based on your starter. Levels range from Lv54–65. Have a diverse team ready.
  • ⭐Watch the credits. You're entered into the Hall of Fame!

13 POST-GAME — CERULEAN CAVE + LEGENDARIES ✨
  • ⭐Cerulean Cave unlocks after beating the Elite Four. Surf to the entrance north of Cerulean City.
  • 🔴Mewtwo (Lv70) waits at the bottom. It's the most powerful Pokémon in the game. Use your Master Ball, or bring max Ultra Balls + sleep/paralyze moves.
  • ⭐Legendary Birds: Articuno (Seafoam Islands), Zapdos (Power Plant), Moltres (Mt. Ember — post-game, Sevii Islands).
  • ⭐Complete the Sevii Islands questline (accessible after beating the Elite Four via the Tri-Pass and Rainbow Pass). Required to trade with Ruby/Sapphire.
  • ▶Sevii Islands contain new Pokémon not found in Kanto — Fire Red and Leaf Green exclusive content.
